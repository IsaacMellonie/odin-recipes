Carrot Cake

Moist Carrot Cake

Try cooking up this carrot cake at home. It's a classic recipe which uses fresh carrots.
There's plenty to go round in this recipe so make sure to store it in the fridge and enjoy later!

  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Carrot
  • Pecan
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Make the batter.
  2. Bake the cake.
  3. Frost the cake.
