Lasagna

Delicious Creamy Lasagna

Lasagna is a great meal to cook for a large group. It's bound to be a hit with all the kids too!
Try this tasty recipe and you'll making this a regular spot on the weekly roster.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the cheese mix.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
